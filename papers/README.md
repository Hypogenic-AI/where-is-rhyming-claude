# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers (Directly Relevant)

1. **[I Have No Mouth, and I Must Rhyme](merullo2025_rhyme_llama.pdf)**
   - Authors: Oliver McLaughlin, Arjun Khurana, Jack Merullo
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2508.02527
   - Why relevant: PRIMARY PAPER - investigates phonetic representations in Llama 3.2, identifies "phoneme mover head", constructs internal vowel chart

2. **[PWESuite: Phonetic Word Embeddings and Tasks They Facilitate](pwesuite_2023.pdf)**
   - Authors: Vilém Zouhar, Kalvin Chang, et al.
   - Year: 2023
   - arXiv: 2304.02541
   - Why relevant: Evaluation suite for phonetic embeddings including rhyme detection; provides benchmark datasets

3. **[Small Language Models Also Work With Small Vocabularies](baby_llamas_2024.pdf)**
   - Authors: Bastian Bunzeck, Daniel Duran, Leonie Schade, Sina Zarrieß
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2410.01487
   - Why relevant: Compares grapheme vs phoneme LMs on rhyme prediction; shows character-level LMs encode phonological info

4. **[Interpreting Character Embeddings With Perceptual Representations](interpreting_char_embeddings_2022.pdf)**
   - Authors: Various
   - Year: 2022
   - Why relevant: Shows character embeddings encode sound information
